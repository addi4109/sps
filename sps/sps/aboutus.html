About Us

About Institute

Satara Polytechnic, Satara is a reputed technical institute committed to providing quality diploma education in engineering and technology. The institute focuses on skill-based learning, practical exposure and overall development of students.

Vision

To become a leading polytechnic institute by delivering quality technical education and developing skilled, innovative and socially responsible professionals.

Mission

  • Provide quality education as per industry needs
  • Strengthen practical training through laboratories and workshops
  • Develop discipline, ethics and professionalism among students
  • Encourage innovation, teamwork and lifelong learning
