About Us

About Institute

Satara Polytechnic, Satara is a well established technical institute in Maharashtra. The institute focuses on quality diploma education and skill oriented training.

Vision

To be a leading polytechnic institute by delivering quality technical education and developing skilled, innovative and socially responsible professionals.

Mission

  • Provide quality education as per industry needs
  • Strengthen practical training through labs and workshops
  • Develop discipline, ethics and professionalism
  • Encourage innovation, teamwork and lifelong learning
