About Institute

Satara Polytechnic, Satara is a well-established technical institute in Maharashtra, committed to providing quality diploma education and skill-oriented training.

The institute focuses on academic excellence, hands-on practical knowledge, discipline, and overall personality development.

Vision

To be a leading polytechnic institute by delivering quality technical education and developing skilled, innovative, and socially responsible professionals.

Mission

  • Quality technical education as per industry needs
  • Strong practical training through labs & workshops
  • Discipline, ethics and professionalism
  • Innovation, teamwork and lifelong learning
