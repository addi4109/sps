Admission Process

Step 1: Eligibility Check

Ensure you meet the eligibility criteria for diploma courses: Passed SSC (10th) for First Year or ITI / HSC for Direct Second Year admission.

Step 2: Online Application

Complete the online application form by filling in personal, academic details and uploading required documents.

Step 3: Document Verification & Merit List

Shortlisted candidates are called for document verification. Merit lists are published based on eligibility and seat availability.

Step 4: Fee Payment & Seat Confirmation

After selection, pay the prescribed fees to confirm your seat. Admission is finalized after completing all formalities.

Step 5: Orientation & Commencement

Attend the orientation programme and begin your academic journey on the announced date.
