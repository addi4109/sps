Admission Process

Step 1: Eligibility Check

Ensure you meet the eligibility criteria for our diploma courses: passed 10th (SSC) or equivalent for first year; or ITI/ HSC for direct second year. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Step 2: Online Application

Visit our website and complete the online application form. Provide accurate personal and academic details and upload required documents.

Step 3: Document Verification & Merit List

After submission, shortlisted candidates are called for document verification. Merit lists are published based on eligibility and availability. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

Step 4: Fee Payment & Seat Confirmation

Upon selection, pay the course fee and confirm your seat. Admission is finalized once all formalities are complete.

Step 5: Orientation & Commencement

Join the orientation programme and start your classes on the announced date.
